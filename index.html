FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What's included in the box?

NVIDIA Jetson Orin device, pre-configured with ŠČYRA.AI software, power adapter, quick-start guide, and access to the online dashboard. Camera and microphone hardware sold separately or available as add-ons.

Does it require internet to work?

No. ŠČYRA.AI runs entirely on the local device — all AI processing happens on-premise. Internet is optional for software updates and cloud analytics sync.

What languages does it support?

Currently: English, German, Belarusian, Russian, Ukrainian, Polish. More languages are added based on backer demand. The system is designed for multilingual European clinics.

When will backers receive their devices?

Estimated delivery: Q4 2026 for Early Bird and Clinic Pack tiers. Franchise Ready tier includes a pre-launch consultation starting within 30 days of campaign close.

Is my patient data safe?

Yes. All data stays on your local device — nothing is sent to external servers. ŠČYRA.AI is designed GDPR-compliant from day one. Sensitive records (PESEL, passport) are stored in encrypted local storage only.

What kind of clinic is this for?

Any dental clinic — from solo practices to multi-chair clinics. The system scales from 1 to 10+ operatory rooms. It's especially effective for clinics that want to automate reception, scheduling, and patient follow-up.

Can I get a refund?

Indiegogo offers a 14-day refund window after the campaign ends. After production begins, refunds follow our standard warranty policy. See our Terms of Service for details.
