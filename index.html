FAQ

Questions? Answers.

Does patient data leave the clinic?
No. SCYRA processes everything on the local Jetson device. No cloud processing, no data transfer. Full GDPR and HIPAA compliance by design.
What hardware is required?
Just a network connection and a power outlet. We ship the pre-configured NVIDIA Jetson AGX Orin with 2TB storage. Setup takes 15 minutes.
Can SCYRA integrate with my current software?
Yes. SCYRA has REST API and supports integration with major practice management systems. Custom integrations available on Enterprise plan.
How accurate is the AI diagnostics?
Our diagnostic models achieve 94%+ accuracy on caries detection and 91%+ on periapical pathology, validated against clinical datasets. AI assists — the doctor decides.
What languages does SCYRA support?
SCYRA speaks 12 languages: Belarusian, German, English, Spanish, French, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, and Ukrainian. Patient communication is automatically localized.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. 30-day free trial with full functionality. We ship the hardware, you try it. If it's not for you, ship it back — no questions asked.
