All AI inference runs on the local Jetson device. X-rays, patient records, facial recognition — everything processed within the building. Internet is optional, not required.

GDPR & HIPAA Native

Compliance isn't a checkbox — it's built into the system architecture. Data residency, right to deletion, access logging, and encryption at rest are defaults, not add-ons.

FAQ

Questions? Answers.

Does patient data ever leave the clinic?
Never. ŠČYRA processes everything on the local Jetson device. No cloud processing, no third-party APIs, no data transfer. Full GDPR and HIPAA compliance is architectural, not configured.
What hardware is included?
NVIDIA Jetson Thor (Blackwell, 128GB), 2TB NVMe SSD, 48TB NAS (4x16TB RAID5), camera integration kit, and pre-installed ŠČYRA software with all 7 agents. Setup takes 15 minutes.
Can it integrate with existing practice management software?
Yes. ŠČYRA exposes a REST API and supports webhooks. We provide integration guides for major dental PMS platforms. Custom integrations available on request.
How accurate is the diagnostic AI?
94%+ accuracy on caries detection, 91%+ on periapical pathology — validated on clinical datasets. The AI provides recommendations; the doctor always makes the final decision.
What happens if the internet goes down?
Nothing changes. ŠČYRA runs entirely on-premise. AI inference, scheduling, patient management, face recognition — all work without internet. Only external messaging (Telegram, WhatsApp) requires connectivity.
What languages are supported?
12 languages: Belarusian, German, English, Spanish, French, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, and Ukrainian. Both the staff interface and patient-facing communication.
How does it work for a franchise network?
Each location gets its own Jetson unit for fully independent local operation. Franchise owners get a centralized dashboard for unified reporting, KPIs, and fleet management — without accessing individual patient data.
Can the hardware be upgraded in the future?
Yes. ŠČYRA's software stack is hardware-agnostic within the Jetson family. As NVIDIA releases next-gen modules, your clinic can upgrade the device while keeping all data, models, and configurations intact.
