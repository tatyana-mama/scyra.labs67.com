Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. Who We Are

ŠČYRA.AI is a product of LABS67. We build edge AI systems for dental clinics. This policy explains how we handle data in our product and on this website.

2. Edge-First Architecture

ŠČYRA.AI is designed with privacy as a core principle. The AI device runs locally in your clinic. Patient data — including images, voice recordings, and medical records — is processed on-device and never transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

3. What Data We Collect

On this website:

  • Basic analytics (page views, referral source) — no personal data
  • Information you voluntarily provide (email for campaign updates)

In the ŠČYRA.AI product:

  • All patient data stays on your local device
  • Sensitive records (PESEL, passport, photos) are stored in encrypted local storage only
  • Optional cloud sync sends only anonymized analytics (appointment counts, usage statistics)
  • Voice recordings are processed in real-time and not stored unless configured by the clinic

4. How We Use Data

  • To provide and improve the ŠČYRA.AI service
  • To send campaign updates (only if you opted in)
  • To generate anonymized usage statistics for product improvement

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. Period. The only exceptions:

  • Legal requirements (court order, regulatory obligation)
  • Service providers who process data on our behalf under strict contracts (e.g., payment processing for Indiegogo)

6. GDPR Compliance

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@labs67.com.

7. Data Security

ŠČYRA.AI uses encryption at rest and in transit. The edge device runs in an isolated environment. Access to clinic data requires local authentication — there is no remote access by default.

8. Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies. No tracking cookies, no ad networks, no third-party analytics that track individuals.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our product evolves. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered users and on this page.
